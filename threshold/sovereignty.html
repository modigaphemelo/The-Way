Sovereignty Check

Before moving from Stage 1 to Stage 2

You've seen what you were given.
You know your ground.
You walk deliberately.
But can you choose your direction without permission?

What Is Sovereignty?

Sovereignty is the ability to say no. To leave. To choose your own direction without asking for permission. It is not wealth or status. It is the foundation that makes everything else possible.

Without sovereignty, you are always walking someone else's path. With it, you can begin to make your own way.

The test

"If I needed to walk away from my current situation, could I?"

The Four Foundations

1

Financial Sovereignty

Six months of expenses. In accessible form. Not in retirement accounts. Not in assets you can't liquidate. Cash or equivalents that let you walk away and not panic.

Emergency fund · Liquid assets · No debt chokehold

Ask yourself: "If my income stopped today, how long could I maintain my direction?"

2

Skill Sovereignty

A skill that doesn't need credentials. Something you can do that the market values, that doesn't require a license, degree, or permission. A portfolio, not a diploma.

Demonstrable ability · Market value · No permission needed

Ask yourself: "If my current job disappeared, could I prove my worth without a certificate?"

3

Relational Sovereignty

At least one non-transactional relationship. Someone who would show up for you even if you had nothing to offer. Not a network contact. Not a mentor who benefits from you. Someone who walks with you, not because of what you provide.

Unconditional support · Mutual care · No transaction

Ask yourself: "Who would still be there if I had nothing to give?"

4

Psychological Sovereignty

The ability to choose without permission. Internally. Not needing validation. Not waiting for approval. The quiet confidence that your direction is yours to choose.

Self-trust · Internal validation · Quiet confidence

Ask yourself: "Do I need others to approve my choices before I trust them?"

The Counterfeit

!

Counterfeit Sovereignty

"I can work anywhere. I'm my own boss. I have no one telling me what to do."

This is often just precarity with a marketing budget. Gig work that offers flexibility but no security. Freelancing that offers freedom but no floor. Independence that means you alone bear every risk.

The test: "Could I lose this and still walk? If the platform changes its algorithm, if the client disappears, if the market shifts—do I still have ground?"

The Decision

Before you move to Stage 2, ask yourself honestly:

  • Do I have six months of expenses saved?
  • Do I have a skill that doesn't need credentials?
  • Do I have at least one non-transactional relationship?
  • Can I choose my direction without seeking permission?

If you answered "no" to any of these: Return to Stage 1. These are not optional luxuries. They are the foundation of sovereignty. Without them, you are walking on borrowed ground.

If you answered "yes" to all: You are ready to make your way. Proceed to Stage 2.
